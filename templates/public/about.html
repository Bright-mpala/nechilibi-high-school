Our Story

A Legacy of Excellence
in Education

Nechilibi High School has been a cornerstone of secondary education in Matabeleland South since 1985. Situated in the heart of Nechilibi, we serve students from Form 1 through Form 6, providing a nurturing environment where every learner can discover their potential.

Our institution follows the ZIMSEC curriculum, offering both O-Level and A-Level programmes. With over four decades of experience, we have built a reputation for academic discipline, strong moral values, and producing graduates who excel at universities and in professional life across Zimbabwe and beyond.

Our Purpose

Vision & Mission

Our Vision

To be a centre of educational excellence that produces well-rounded, innovative, and morally upright citizens who contribute positively to Zimbabwe and the world.

Our Mission

To provide quality, accessible secondary education that empowers students academically, socially, and spiritually — building a strong foundation for lifelong success.

What We Stand For

Our Core Values

The principles that guide everything we do at Nechilibi High School.

Excellence

We strive for the highest academic and personal standards in everything we do, pushing every student to reach their full potential.

Integrity

Honesty and accountability are at the heart of our school culture. We build young people of strong character and moral uprightness.

Respect

We uphold the dignity of every person in our community — students, teachers, parents, and the broader society.

Teamwork

We believe in collaboration among students, teachers, and parents. Together we create an environment where everyone thrives.

Innovation

We embrace new and creative approaches to learning and teaching, preparing students for an ever-changing world.

Service

We instill a spirit of community service and civic responsibility, producing graduates who give back to their communities.

What We Offer

Academic Programmes

Two structured academic pathways under the ZIMSEC curriculum.

O-Level (Forms 1–4)

A broad, well-rounded curriculum preparing students for ZIMSEC O-Level examinations. Students study core and elective subjects across Science, Arts, Commercial and Technical streams.

Mathematics Sciences Languages Commerce Humanities Practicals

A-Level (Forms 5–6)

Advanced level studies preparing students for university entrance. Students specialise in subject combinations across Sciences, Humanities, and Commercial streams under ZIMSEC.
