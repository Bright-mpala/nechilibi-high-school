Our Story

Nechilibi High School is a secondary school located in Matabeleland South, Zimbabwe. The school provides quality education to students from Form 1 through Form 6, preparing them for ZIMSEC O-Level and A-Level examinations.

Our institution is built on a foundation of academic excellence, strong moral values, and holistic development. We believe every student has the potential to succeed and our dedicated staff work tirelessly to bring out the best in each learner.

Situated in the heart of Matabeleland South, we serve students from the surrounding communities, offering both boarding and day school options to ensure access to quality education for all.

Our Vision

To be a centre of educational excellence that produces well-rounded, innovative, and morally upright citizens who contribute positively to Zimbabwe and the world.

Our Mission

To provide quality, accessible secondary education that empowers students academically, socially, and spiritually, building a strong foundation for lifelong success.

Our Core Values

  • Excellence — We strive for the highest academic and personal standards in everything we do.
  • Integrity — Honesty and accountability are at the heart of our school culture.
  • Respect — We uphold the dignity of every person in our community.
  • Teamwork — We believe in collaboration among students, teachers, and parents.
  • Innovation — We embrace new approaches to learning and teaching.
  • Service — We instill a spirit of community service and civic responsibility.

Our Programmes

O-Level (Forms 1–4)

A broad curriculum leading to ZIMSEC O-Level examinations with 17+ subjects offered.

A-Level (Forms 5–6)

Advanced level studies preparing students for university and professional careers.
