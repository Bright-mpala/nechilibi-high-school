About Our School

Nechilibi High School is a leading secondary school located in Matabeleland South, Zimbabwe. We are committed to providing quality education that prepares students for the challenges of the modern world.

Our school follows the ZIMSEC curriculum, offering O-Level (Forms 1-4) and A-Level (Forms 5-6) programmes. We pride ourselves on academic excellence, strong moral values, and holistic development of every student.

Academic Programmes

We offer a comprehensive ZIMSEC curriculum from Form 1 to Form 6

O-Level (Forms 1–4)

Comprehensive foundation education covering Sciences, Arts, and Commerce. Preparing students for ZIMSEC O-Level examinations.

A-Level (Forms 5–6)

Advanced level studies for university preparation. Specialized subjects in Sciences, Humanities, and Commercial streams.

Co-Curricular

Sports, music, drama, and clubs that develop well-rounded students with leadership skills and team spirit.
