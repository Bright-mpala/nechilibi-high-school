How to Apply

We welcome applications from academically motivated students who wish to pursue their secondary education at Nechilibi High School. Our admissions process is straightforward and transparent.

4

Enrolment

Successful applicants complete enrolment and fee payment

Entry Requirements

FormEntry Requirements
Form 1Grade 7 certificate with at least 5 passes including English and Mathematics
Form 2Successful completion of Form 1 or equivalent from another school

Form 5 (Lower 6th)Minimum 5 O-Level passes at Grade C or better, including English Language
Form 6 (Upper 6th)Successful completion of Form 5 / Lower 6th

Documents Required

  • Completed application form (download from Downloads page)
  • Certified copy of birth certificate
  • Latest school report / end-of-year results
  • Certified copies of relevant certificates (O-Level results for Form 5 entry)
  • Two recent passport-size photographs
  • Transfer letter (for students transferring from another school)
  • Medical / health form (available from the school office)
Note: All certified copies must be certified by a Commissioner of Oaths or Notary Public. Original documents must be presented on enrolment day.
